@ Yeshua High School

Shaping minds, building character, and creating future leaders since 2005.

Our Story

Founded in 2005 with just 120 students, Yeshua High School has grown into one of the region's most prestigious educational institutions, serving over 700 students from preschool through high school.

Our founder, Mrs Simisola Adigun, envisioned a school that would combine academic excellence with character development. Today, we continue to uphold his vision while adapting to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our 25-acre campus features state-of-the-art facilities, including science and technology labs, performance arts spaces, and athletic complexes that support our comprehensive educational approach.

Our Journey Through Time

2005

Yeshua High School opens its doors with 120 students in grades K-5. The school is founded by Mrs Simisola Adigun, a visionary educator.

2010

The school expands to include middle school grades, responding to community demand for continued quality education.

2015

High school division is added, completing the K-12 program. Our first senior class graduates in 2005.

2020

State-of-the-art science and technology center opens, featuring labs, makerspaces, and innovation studios.

2025

Yeshua High School is recognized as a National red Ribbon School for academic excellence.
